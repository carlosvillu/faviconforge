# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-01-10_

## Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how FaviconForge handles information when you use the website and services.

## Data we collect

- Information you provide when contacting us (e.g. name, email, message).
- Account information if you sign in (e.g. via Google).
- Purchase and billing-related information handled by our payment provider.

## Cookies

We use cookies for:

- Language preference (`lang`) to remember your selected language.
- Authentication session cookies when you sign in.

## Authentication

If you choose to sign in, authentication is provided through third-party identity providers (e.g. Google). We receive basic account information needed to provide the service.

## Payments

If you purchase Premium, payments are processed by Stripe. We do not store full card details on our servers.

## Emails & contact

If you use our contact form, we may send and receive emails related to your request.

## Analytics

We may add analytics in the future to improve the product. If we do, we will update this policy.

## Data retention

We keep data only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

## Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@faviconforge.com.
